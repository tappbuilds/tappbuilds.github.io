About Chris Tapp

Chris Tapp loves to create and build cool things, especially outside. He has more than 20 years of experience in landscaping, working on both residential and commercial projects. He specializes in pondless waterscapes, synthetic turf putting greens, rock/retaining walls and landscape design/installation. He enjoys collaborating with clients, helping bring their outdoor spaces to life. Chris has created custom design features in Indiana, Utah, Minnesota, Washington, Illinois and North Carolina.
